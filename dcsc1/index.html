Formation Activities

The Diocesan Commission shall hold formation activities quarterly during the year that shall also serve as assemblies of social communications ministers in the Diocese.

Formation and Orientation towards a Catholic Understanding of Social Communications (FOCUS)

This will serve as orientation to new members or associate members of Parish Commissions on the basic principles of Social Communications and the ministries it performs. For active and returning members, this will serve as a refresher. The goals, programs and activities of the Diocesan Commission for the whole year will be presented in this gathering.

Desiring, Encountering, and Entrusting God‚Äôs Presence in Evangelization (DEEPEN)

This will serve as orientation to new members or associate members of Parish Commissions on the basic principles of Social Communications and the ministries it performs. For active and returning members, this will serve as a refresher. The goals, programs and activities of the Diocesan Commission for the whole year will be presented in this gathering.

Skills Harnessing for Pastoral Ministry and Evangelization (SHARPEN)

This will address the need to equip social communications ministers with skills necessary for the ministry. For those who are already skilled, this may be an avenue to develop their skills further or explore other skills that they may learn. This is also an avenue for sharing of knowledge and experiences among ministers.

Proclaiming the Charisms, Life, Achievements, and Inspirations in the Ministry (PROCMAIM)

Set in the last quarter of the year, this will be a yearend gathering of social communications ministers, media partners and other collaborators in the ministry to celebrate the fruits of the ministry and collaboration. This is also an opportunity to recognize people and works through giving of awards and certificates to inspire everyone to excel in the ministry in the service of the Church and for the Glory of God.
